About

Since our launch in 2007, our vision and focus has been to deliver high value projects for our clients with the emphasis on communication, attention to detail and cost management.

We understand the importance of innovation and professionalism and work with the best people to achieve this.

Importantly, we have fun doing it.

When Stonewood Construction was first conceived we set out to build a business model that focused on quality and attention to detail. We soon realised the difficulty in sustaining this model as a startup in the competitive environment of construction, but we persisted and stuck to our ideals. Today we operate with the confidence that our past has prepared us to embrace the most challenging of projects. Our clients and architects alike receive the benefit of the energy we put into our management and site teams.
